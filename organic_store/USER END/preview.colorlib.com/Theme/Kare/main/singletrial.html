KEY BENEFITS

Fatty acids: Avocado oil mainly consists of Palmitic Acid, Palmitoleic Acid, Stearic Acid, Oleic Acid, Linoleic Acid, Alpha-linolenic Acid, Arachidic Acid, and Gadoleic Acid. It is moisturising and nourishing for the skin, scalp, and hair. The oil also strengthens the Stratum Corneum, preventing Transepidermal Water Loss. 

Plant vitamins: Avocado oil contains vitamins A, B6, C, D, and E. Vitamin C is an antioxidant that stimulates collagen production. 

Minerals:  The oil contains minerals such as Magnesium and Calcium. 

Antioxidants: Avocado oil is rich in phenols, carotenoids, and other antioxidants. Antioxidants protect against free radical damage. 

More Info

Avocado oil is suitable for: 

  • Skin type - Suitable for dry, rough, aging, sensitive, mature or irritated skin.
  • Gender - Unisex product
  • Expecting and Breastfeeding mothers - Please always check with your Doctor prior to use.

Comedogenic Rating – 2 - 3
(0 – Not likely to clog pores; 1 – Low; 2 – Moderately Low; 3 – Moderate; 4 – Fairly High; 5 – High) 

Since Avocado oil has a comedogenic value of between 2 – 3, depending on your skin type it may cause moderate to moderately low clogging of pores.   

PATCH TEST is advisable before adding a new product to your routine. Apply the product on your inner elbow / jawline and observe for the next 24 hours. If there’s no reaction, you can apply the product on your face. If skin is sensitive, continue the test for 7 days. This helps ensure you are not allergic to any ingredient. If irritation occurs, please discontinue the product and consult a dermatologist.

Additional Notes
×

Our pure, natural and organic oils are undiluted, unrefined and cold pressed. They are strictly free of mineral oil or petroleum derivatives to improve shelf life. Most vegetable oils oxidize over time and eventually become rancid. Keep them away from moisture or any kind of water contamination.

When you pour the oil into your palm each time, make sure that your palms are dry, so as to not let any kind of water come in contact with oil. For example pure, unrefined Coconut Oil can get easily rancid with slightest of water or moisture contamination. You need to make sure that the cap is closed tightly after each use.

Since they are all natural and preservative free, to achieve the best potency please use our oils within 4 - 5 months after opening. You may refrigerate them to extend the shelf life after opening. Place the bottle towards the back of the refrigerator, away from the door to avoid outside temp changes affecting it. Oils might turn cloudy due to refrigeration, but not to worry their quality will remain unaffected. Just place the bottle in a bowl of warm water to regain consistency. DO NOT PUT OIL in FREEZER!!!

We request you to reuse the bottles as much as possible. A few good washes with soap and warm water will take the greasiness of the oil normally away. Dry them well and bottles would be ready for the next use.
